Gödel's theorems

Biology stands for chemistry, chemistry satands for physics and physics stands for mathematics. All mathematics are built up from axioms, statements that are too obvius and are considered objectively true. Though, Kurt Gödel has proven the two Gödel's incompleteness theorems which states that any system must be sustained by statements that cannot be proven in the system. This is huge, because it shows that even the most fundamental part of sciencie may be wrong and we do not have the tools to check it.
