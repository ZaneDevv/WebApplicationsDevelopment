Motivation

You, while reading this, might think that almost everything is already known in our universe. However, there are quite a few gaps in our understanding of it.
The scientists's role in society is to wonder about how everything in the universe work like they do and why, but after all the centuries in the history of science we have left behind so far, we cannot even ensure that the very basis is actually correct or, in case it is not, how we can see it. To top it off, some scientists now are starting to take seriously some topics that, before, were considered "pseudo-science" and anyone that dears to study them should be despised.


In order to understand how big the problem actually is, it is important to know that the famous italian scientist Galileo Galilei, after seeing that there is something we did not even know where to start from, decided to study first the mathematizable things, such as motion, and then all the unmathematizable things such as consciousness. Nonetheless, after 500 years, we are stuck on those mathematizable things in such a way that we name the people that belive that there is something else as "conspiracy theorists" although even Isaac Newton studied the bible in order to find answers or even when the universe will end.


Let me ask you something: why are you alive? What is that thing that distinguish you from an ordinary object? You might answer "consciousness", but what is that? Where does it come from? Why does it even exsit at all?


The world where we live in is much more extrange as we might expect at frist glance, and the last century's breakthroughs proves it with bended spacetime, imaginary time and void that is not empty.
